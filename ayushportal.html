This portal aims to provide information about Ayurveda, Yoga, and other traditional Indian healthcare practices. AYUSH is an acronym for Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy and are the six Indian systems of medicine prevalent and practiced in India and some of the neighboring Asian countries with very few exceptions in some of the developed countries.

About Us

We are dedicated to promoting and preserving the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda and Yoga. Our team consists of experts in these fields who are passionate about sharing their knowledge.The Ayush Portal is a startup aimed at providing a platform for Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy (AYUSH) practitioners, enthusiasts, and seekers to connect, learn, and access resources related to alternative medicine and holistic wellness. The portal may offer services such as online consultations, educational content, community forums, and access to Ayurvedic products
